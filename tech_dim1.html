What are the political, economic, bodily, labour, and historical dimensions of these technologies?

Migration of workers raises various political issues. It becomes the responsibility of the govt to provide employment, safety and accommodation to the migrants. Economy is halted due to lock-downs. Unemployment and poverty make the situation worse.

How does this matter?

This matters as it involves various dimensions of the society be it labour, material, technological, political, economic, bodily, historic, educational or symbolic.
