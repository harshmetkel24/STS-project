BODILY/ORGANIC DIMENSIONS

How are bodies related to it?

There are people so there are bodies and hence there is an organic dimension to the issue.
There are elements of sight, touch, hearing, smell and taste. There is the sight of the plight of the poor workers forced to abandon and leave their place. There are sounds of fear and agony. There is the smell of misery and deprivation. There is this virus ready to infect you. There is the taste of the bitter consequences of getting infected.

What forms of attention, affect, emotion, and cognition are involved?

There is attention from the world. There is attention from the public. In the case of humans, as the picture suggests, In the families of migrant workers, they highly depend on their daily wages and as a result all the future plans, dreams, emotional and mental stability of the family members depend on the payscale and the employment of the worker, and thus they were highly affected on both emotional and physical terms because of this pandemic scapegoat.
Most of the construction or similar organisations have layers of working, the upper layer that conducts the board meetings and carries out the decisions , the mid layer where all the office work relies and the lower layer where all the physical work is being done by the workers. We all know that the most affected category in these layers are the workers. And they don't have backup finance and also they all rely on the daily pay.

Are there particular ways in which we think of ourselves that also involve or sustain this process?

In case we own or have power of decision in a similar organisation then as in the covid era the income had been lower than the deepest valley, all we could do is to cut off some of the workers so that we could sustain our company and the lower category was the easiest to let lose because the middle office work layer would require resources of interview, internships etc for re establishment while this lowest layer does not require such a high amount of resources to re establish even post covid.
In case we are normal citizens. Then because of this covid era we shifted our sentiments and our priorities. We shifted to things of basic requirement and thus a major chunk of the companies had to lose their revenue for those 1 or two years. Especially real estate and the automobile were the two such sectors that were affected a lot.
Now as normal citizens , whose job is either paused or income is reduced then we try to minimize our costs of living and this affects the companies that provide employment to these workers and thus we enhance this problem , as a mutual side effect of our attempts. Thus the methods that we adopted were necessary and important for us but they proved to be drastic and lethal for others.

What kinds of bodies, including nonhumans, and bodily relations are involved in producing it?

In the covid era we truly experienced the notion of “The world is round” . Every factor that led to this atrocity was necessary and important to be done by people or organisation.Organisations incurring huge losses because of grounded demands of their products. People who reduce the demand in order to establish their low cost of living.

What kinds make use of it?

Travel and transportation is one of the bodies that made huge use of this situation for its profit. The migrant workers had to move to their hometown and they, being not able to afford trains or flights , relied on the local road transport. The prices of the tickets ranged somewhere between 5 to 10 times their normal price. As the workers would have no other option but to buy the tickets, the travels and transportation made huge profits.
Another industry that made huge use of this situation is the life dependencies and the medical industry. Life dependencies like grains, sugar, vegetables etc were sold at 2 to 3 times their original prices and the things of basic requirement in covid, masks, sanitisers , wipes etc were sold at a price 5 to 10 times their original price and due to huge spike in their demand. As every person relies on life dependencies and in the covid era, every soul had to rely on medical facilities as well, these industries made huge wins.

Are there racial, gendered, differently abled, or other group identifications that help construct these bodies?

Racism:
In most of the indian cities and areas, migrant workers are mostly of lower caste as suggested by the indian system. And the people who are born in lower castes are always associated with such physical and low level jobs.

Gendered:
even though the situations have improved in recent times but still a majority of the workers doing physical work consists of male. The proportion of women has increased tremendously in the office work segment but not in the lowest layer that consists of physical workers.

Education:
The low skilled and less educated segment of the society is usually associated with such jobs. Because the importance of education and degrees is rising day by day, we can say that not having proper education makes a perception of a person in the minds of people that he has to be associated with this kind of work.

What ways of life are involved?

The pandemic affects the health directly and thus it affects every aspect of life because we have to limit our routine and our priorities in order to prevent catching the disease and if we do catch it then we have to halt everything till we recover from it.

What are the histories of all these relations?

The history shows that whenever a pandemic occurs, the population at the lowest strata i.e the daily wage workers have suffered the most.
